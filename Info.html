Before using our product!

  • KEEP AWAY FROM CHILDREN
  • Use only as directed. Misuse may result in injury or ineffective protection.
  • Do not apply to eyes, mouth, open wounds, or broken skin.
  • Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes. In case of contact, rinse thoroughly with water
  • Do not ingest. If swallowed, seek medical help or contact a Poison Control Center immediately
  • Not recommended for use on children under age of 3 without consulting a doctor.
  • If irritation, rash, or allergic reaction occurs, discontinue use and consult a physician.
  • Keep away from heat, sparks, open flames, and hot surfaces. No smoking near product.
  • Do not apply under clothing or on excessively sunburned or damaged skin.
  • Do not use near food, drink, or animal feed.
  • Store in a cool, dry place, out of direct sunlight.
  • Before using "BIOSHIELD please perform a skin graph test before applying on whole body to test for any signs of irritation or Alergic reaction
